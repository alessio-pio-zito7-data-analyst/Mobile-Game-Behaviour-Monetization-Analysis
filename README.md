# Mobile Game Behaviour Monetization Analysis

1. Business Context

A mobile game publisher is preparing the launch of several new titles throughout 2025. Before defining future monetization strategies, the company wants to better understand player engagement and in-app purchasing behavior across its existing player base.

The objective is to identify the characteristics of highly engaged and highly profitable players, understand spending patterns across different demographics and devices, and evaluate whether current in-app purchase strategies should be expanded, optimized, or reduced in future game releases.
